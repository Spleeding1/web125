Services

Have you been driving through the rain or snow and noticed that your tires aren’t gripping like they used too? Have you noticed a noise that occurs every time you step on the brakes? Has a warning light come on and you have no idea why? No matter the concern, we will fix it!

About Us

In 1999, Carl (our founder) started his first job as an automotive technician. For the next 8 years, he worked for different repair shops, noticing a trend between them:
A lack of integrity.

Seeing customers become frustrated with not knowing why repairs and services were being recommended, he ventured on a new endeavor, making the other’s greatest weakness his greatest strength. Customers were skeptical at first, but Carl’s Auto Repair quickly gained the trust of many. 12 years later, there are over 30 people dedicated to making sure you are treated the right way.
